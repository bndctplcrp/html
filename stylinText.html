A paragraph is defined as “a group of sentences or a single sentence that forms a unit” (Lunsford and Connors 116). Length and appearance do not determine a section in a paper is a paragraph. For instance, in some of writing , particularly journalistic styles, a paragraph can be just one sentence long.
